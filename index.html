01 — Logo & Symbol

The GeoBee mark is a single hive cell that has become a bee — the atom of the brand's language. This document specifies how to write the name, reproduce the mark, colour it, space it, and export it. Every rule here protects the mark's clarity from a favicon to a building sign.

1.1 — Writing the name

GeoBee, always one word

The name is written as a single closed compound with two internal capitals — a medial capital on both stems. This spelling is the trademark and must never be altered, spaced, hyphenated, or fully capitalised in running text.

✓ Correct
GeoBee GeoBee.io GeoBee™ (first / prominent use)
✕ Never
Geobee · geobee · GEOBEE Geo Bee · Geo-Bee · geoBee GeoBee.IO · geobee.io · GeoBee .io

Exception: all-caps lock-ups (e.g. a nav bar set entirely in capitals) may render GEOBEE for optical consistency, but the medial capitals return the moment mixed case is available. Show the ™ on first or most prominent mention; switch to ® once the mark is registered.

1.2 — The logo system

Three assets, two colourways

The system is deliberately small: a primary lockup, a symbol, and a text wordmark. Each exists in a light-mode (ink on paper) and dark-mode (paper on ink) rendering. The yellow never changes.

Use the primary lockup wherever space allows. Drop to the symbol for avatars, app icons and favicons. Use the text wordmark only in single-colour or tightly constrained contexts (embossing, fax-grade print, legal footers).

1.3 — Construction & reproduction

Building the bee from scratch

1.7 — Typography in the mark

Sora & Space Mono

Aa
Sora — SemiBold

The wordmark is set in Sora SemiBold, letter-spacing −0.03em. A geometric sans — calm, modern, engineered. Also the brand's display/headline face.

.io
Space Mono — Bold

The .io suffix and all technical / data labels use Space Mono — a nod to the telemetry and coordinates the platform runs on.

Both are open-source Google Fonts, so the mark can be rebuilt on any machine. Body & UI copy uses Figtree — see document 03.

1.8 — File formats & export

Which file, where

SVGMaster format. Use everywhere on the web and in any vector workflow — infinitely scalable, tiny, editable. The source of truth.
PNGTransparent raster for slides, docs & email. Export at 1× / 2× / 3×; supply 512 & 1024 px symbol tiles for app stores.
JPGOnly where transparency is impossible (some ad networks). Always on Paper White #FAF8F4 — never a coloured or photographic bed.
ICOWindows / browser favicon. Symbol only, multi-resolution (16 / 32 / 48 px). Generate from the 48 px PNG.
PDFVector print master for stationery & large-format. Hand to any printer; embed CMYK / PMS as specified in 1.6.

Naming convention: geobee_[lockup|symbol|wordmark]_[light|dark]_[size].[ext] — e.g. geobee_symbol_dark_512.png. Each colourway ships on both light and dark grounds.

02 — Brand Guidelines

Who GeoBee is, what it believes, and how it shows up. This is the master reference — the logo, colour and type specifics live in their own documents (01 & 03); here we set the principles that everything else answers to.

2.1 — Purpose

We turn risk into something a person can calmly act on.

Mission

To give every worker, team and organisation calm, real-time awareness of the risks around their people, assets and places — and the means to act before harm occurs.

Vision

A world where no one works unseen or unprotected — where safety is ambient, shared, and native to how organisations operate.

2.2 — The organising metaphor

The hive is the model

The bumblebee and its hive aren't decoration — they are how GeoBee thinks and speaks. The whole product lexicon derives from this one idea. (Full glossary in document 04.)

The Bee

A single worker, or an asset — anything of value to protect. Small, capable, never alone.

The Hive

A team, division or whole organisation — a meaningful grouping of bees that shares awareness & protection.

The Guardian

GeoBee itself — the instinct of the hive to watch, warn and protect its own. Duty, made operational.

2.3 — Brand principles

Five commitments

01
Calm over concern

We reduce anxiety, never manufacture it. We inform and enable action; we do not alarm. Tone is a steady hand, not a siren.

02
Protect the individual

Every bee matters. The lone worker gets the same care as the enterprise. Safety is never a privilege of scale.

03
Clarity is safety

In a risk moment, ambiguity is dangerous. We design for the fastest possible understanding — plain words, clear signal, no clutter.

04
Earn trust, keep it

We handle people's location and safety data. We are transparent, precise, and never careless with that responsibility.

05
Stronger together

Awareness shared across a hive protects everyone in it. The network is the product; collective safety beats individual vigilance.

2.4 — Personality

We are, we are not

We are
Calm Vigilant Warm Precise Dependable Grounded
We are not
Alarmist Cold / clinical Militaristic Hype-driven Fear-selling

The full voice system, do/don't phrasing and the hive lexicon are in document 04 · Brand Voice & Lexicon.

04 — Brand Voice & Lexicon

How GeoBee sounds, and the words it uses. The voice keeps calm over concern in every sentence; the lexicon borrows from the hive so the product, the docs and the sales conversation all speak one coherent language.

4.1 — Voice pillars

Four dials, always set the same way

Steady, not urgent

We write like the calm voice on the radio in a crisis. Short, clear, composed — even when the news is serious.

Warm, not soft

Human and kind, but never vague. Warmth earns trust; precision keeps it. We care, and we're exact.

Plain, not dumbed-down

Everyday words for everyday people; exact terms when precision matters. We respect the reader's time and intelligence.

Confident, not boastful

We show competence through clarity, not superlatives. No hype, no fear-selling, no “revolutionary”.

4.2 — Say this, not that

Calm over concern, in practice

✓ We say
“Aisha checked in safe at 14:03.”
✕ Not
“No fatalities reported at site 14.”
✓ We say
“Raised risk near the north gate. Here's what to do.”
✕ Not
“⚠ DANGER! Threat detected immediately!!”
✓ We say
“Your hive is fully monitored. All 12 bees are accounted for.”
✕ Not
“Surveillance active on all 12 assets.”
✓ We say
“Let's get you set up in a few minutes.”
✕ Not
“Complete mandatory onboarding to activate protection.”

4.4 — The hive lexicon

The domain language

These are canonical product terms. Capitalise them when naming the concept in-product (“invite a Bee to your Hive”); lowercase in ordinary prose. Use consistently — this vocabulary is a brand asset.

BeeAn individual worker or a protected asset — the atomic unit of value.
HiveA team, site, division or whole organisation — a semantic grouping of Bees.
QueenThe critical asset or person a Hive exists to protect; continuity depends on it.
CellA defined zone or geofence — one cell of the monitored map.
ForageA Bee working out in the field, beyond the home Hive — lone/remote work.
WaggleA check-in or status signal a Bee sends back to the Hive.
GuardianThe GeoBee watch layer — the always-on protective intelligence.
StingAn escalation / SOS — a Bee's defensive alert when threatened.
SwarmCoordinated response — resources converging on an incident.
PollenThe signal data Bees carry back — telemetry that feeds the map.
NectarRefined insight — the analysed intelligence GeoBee produces from Pollen.
BeekeeperA manager / admin who oversees a Hive from the command view.

Use with a light touch in customer-facing copy — the metaphor should aid understanding, never obscure it. When in doubt, plain language wins.

4.5 — Speaking to each audience

One voice, four rooms

Lone workers

Reassurance & simplicity. “You're never really alone out there.” Lead with personal safety, not features.

SMEs

Practicality & value. “Enterprise-grade protection, set up in an afternoon.” Speak to duty of care without the overhead.

NGOs

Mission & care. “Protect the people who protect others.” Emphasise field safety, dignity and low-connectivity resilience.

Enterprise

Rigour & trust. “Observability and control across every hive.” Speak to compliance, scale and continuity — calmly, never boastfully.
